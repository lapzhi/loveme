Modern families and frequent divorce: how to live with it

Have people stopped trying to resolve conflicts and build a strong and lasting Alliance? Divorce has become the norm, and more and more people are not ready to be in a failed relationship. In the CIS countries, approximately 50% of families break up.

So why and when did divorce become the norm?

Scientists have found that marriage, on average, lasts about 10 years. Common reasons for divorce: infidelity, lack of understanding, and financial problems.

Because of their emotionality, women are more likely to speak out initiators of the divorce. They are more likely to experience failures and conflicts in the family.

People are getting divorced more often because it's easy to live alone these days. Previously, the family was created for survival. Even 100 years ago, man was not able to feed himself. Now both men and women are able to earn a living and survive on your own.

There are singles who, instead of creating a family and finding common ground, prefer to live and farm alone.

Quite logically, if a woman works, she is not afraid to stay without her husband and will not tolerate him humiliations.

The second significant reason for divorce is high life expectancy. The average life expectancy is growing and it is increasingly difficult to imagine a Union for life. People fall in love, get married, have children. After 10-20 years, interests and spouses change they grow cold to each other and break off their relationship.

Therefore, sociologists increasingly mention serial monogamy - a person during life enters into several monogamous unions, remaining faithful, being in each of them.

No matter what anyone says, everyone wants to be loyal, loved and respected. Let and not for a lifetime.

Marriages for life were made when the average life expectancy was several times less.

There are more requirements for a potential mate

Relationships have become necessary not for survival, but for doing good things. life is better. We began to pay attention to the partner's social status, goals and dreams. If there is no development in the marriage, there is no improvement, we divorce and move on.

Family ties are still valuable to us, but it is equally important who we live with, how comfortable and easy it is to live in everyday life.

And more opportunities for acquaintance..

With the advent of Dating sites, finding the right person has become easier than ever. Interest-based communities, social networks, and apps have expanded opportunities to meet interesting people. Also, there are more specialized conferences, seminars and meetings. People began to travel more often.

Earlier we met with the help of friends or at work (study). It is possible to meet your partner on the street, on public transport, or in a bar.

Thanks to the Internet, Dating has become available at any time of the day. And if you know a foreign language , the whole world is open to you the world!

Are people no longer ready to keep their families together?

No, family is still important. People have become more discerning, their views and expectations of their partner have changed. At the same time, we have become more free in our choice. Women and men are no longer ready to hold on to the an unhappy marriage just because it should be once and for all.

People are not ready to keep a family for the sake of children or because of lack of finances, there is no longer a fear of being left alone.

You don't need to look at the statistics and be afraid of the number of divorces. Marriage and family formation are for happiness and comfort, not for despair.
