8 topics that are best not discussed by text MESSAGE

TEXT messages and messages in messengers have long since replaced phone conversations. But sometimes you send a message and immediately begin to regret what you wrote. Or you can't get up the courage to talk face-to-face, so you send texts instead. There are themes that are better do not discuss it in a text MESSAGE or over the phone.

We have compiled a list of topics that are best discussed in person.

1. Don't complain or reproach by SMS

"You don't treat my parents well," "I'm tired of you not giving me flowers." The interlocutor does not hears your tone and has no idea if you are angry, upset, or making an innocent comment. Talk about the pain during the meeting.

2. Don't blame

Accusations spoil the mood and again, the interlocutor will not understand how serious everything is. Accusations are not the best way to Express dissatisfaction. Not accuse please only talk about yourself and what is unpleasant for you personally.

3. Don't apologize during the conversation

If you have offended or offended your partner, wait for the meeting and sincerely apologize. Yes, this requires courage and courage, but admitting guilt is worth it in real life, not by TEXT message.

4. Questions about behavior

"Are you angry? Why didn't you call me back?" Let the person answer these questions in person. During correspondence, you may misinterpret his explanations.
