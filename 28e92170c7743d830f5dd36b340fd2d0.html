How to communicate on the first date

Even if you have been chatting on a Dating site for a long time, it is quite possible that on the first date you will be shy, and you will forget all the topics for conversation. We have collected 5 fresh tips for communicating on the first dates that will definitely not let you down.

1. It is better to talk about travel

Psychologists have long found that couples who discuss travel on the first date are more likely to meet again than movies and books. Of those who talked about movies, only 9% wanted to meet again. Couples who discussed travel, more willing to agree to a second date-18%.

According to psychologists, this is due to the fact that when discussing holidays and other countries, our eyes light up. We share our experiences and impressions, enthusiastically talking about the past vacation, which makes us more attractive in the eyes of our companion. Check and be sure to tell us in the comments how far the conversation went.

2. Avoid extremes

Avoid extremes during the conversation. Don't be passive, but don't try to dominate. Listen to the other person, be genuinely interested, and ask questions. Show me what you're interested in. The secret to a successful conversation simple: interest and correction of the topic.

3. Feel free to talk about yourself

Many people are embarrassed to talk about themselves. According to psychologists, even on the first date, you can get close to a new acquaintance, if you are quite Frank.

Discuss a list of topics that you will talk about in advance during the meeting, and a list of taboo topics that are best left out for closer acquaintance. You can take turns asking each other selected questions, such as: did you have a dream? did you want to be famous? what is your best memory?

Such a game has an impact fantastic effect. Studies have shown that within an hour, new acquaintances felt closer to each other.

Based on the conclusions of psychologists, some couples have such a level of intimacy appears only after years.

4. Don't be afraid of the controversial ones that

In one of our articles, we wrote that controversial topics (for example, about attitudes to abortion) help us better understand a person and their views on life.

If the conversation has reached a dead end, do not be afraid to start a conversation on an unusual and difficult topic. The main thing is not to reach a conflict.

Ask about something serious, discuss it, and at the same time check how the new acquaintance behaves during arguments, how open and able to take into account someone else's point of view. A conversation that is boring will immediately become interesting.

5. How to understand that a first date is the rest of it?

Life hack. If you expect to continue the evening in a more intimate setting, ask the interlocutor the question: do you Like beer?

According to one American Dating site, regardless of gender or orientation, beer drinkers are 60% more likely to have sex on the first date, as opposed to having sex on the first date. those who don't drink beer. Check it out and tell us how it works!
