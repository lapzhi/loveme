5 original ideas for a Valentine's day date

There are 4 days left until Valentine's Day. Everyone is talking about gifts, discounts and romance. We also decided to keep up, after all, this is our favorite holiday. We have prepared 5 original ideas for Valentine's day dates. And a gift at the end of the article. ;-)

1. Party in the style of the 80's

Such parties are not uncommon. They are always fun, lots of different people and play hits of the 70s and 80s. The atmosphere is better than that of a club. Wear a dress or suit in a retro style and invite your partner to a similar party. Date it will give you a lot of emotions and laughter. By the way, if there is no partner, do not sit alone. Go to the party, in case fate is waiting for you there.

2. Date-tasting

Visit the festival of wine or cheese, themed cuisine. Arrange a tasting date. You can try out a variety of drinks and snacks to accompany them. Only book your seat in advance. At the same time, there will be another reason for discussion.

3. Evening of poetry or classical music

Only if you two are interested, otherwise you will get bored together and the evening will be ruined. Visit a literary evening or a Philharmonic hall. If you can come up with your own poems, end the date with your own work.

4. Travel

The most cool and romantic idea. Of course, it is unlikely to be suitable for a first date, but if you have known each other long enough, invite your partner to travel. One-day excursion to a nearby town or city a romantic weekend where you have long wanted to visit. Create a route and go on a joint adventure.

5. Festival

Choose something interesting for both of you: a festival of movies, music, coffee and sweets. Learn each other's tastes and charge yourself with new emotions.

We hope that these 5 ideas will be enough to make Valentine's Day pass positively and pleasantly. What are your plans? Tell us in the comments.
