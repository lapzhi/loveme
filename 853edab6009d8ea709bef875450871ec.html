8 original places for a second date ðŸ¤—

The first date wasn't always perfect: you were both blushing, embarrassed, your palms sweating, and there was an awkward silence in the air.. But if you understand that you like each other and want to continue communication and acquaintance, feel free make a second appointment. On the second date, the probability of getting screwed up is much lower.

We have collected 8 original places for the second date. Give this acquaintance a second chance!

1. Walk

There is nothing unusual about walking. We Agree. But it is walking that helps you get to know your companion better. There is no need to sit and look at each other, feeling shy and confused.

Choose the most beautiful Park in the city, take a thermos of tea and sandwiches. Walk around the Park, get to know each other, and at the end have a picnic.

2. Available in concert or performance

I charge concerts with positive emotions. It is difficult to talk when music is playing loudly, so you will have to bend over to your partner to say something or discuss it. This helps you feel a special closeness and realize that you came to the event together. After the concert, you can discuss whether you liked it or not, what emotions you have experienced, and you will not have to invent a topic for conversation.

3. Favorite cafe

It is better not to go to your favorite cafe on your first date. Otherwise, if the meeting goes badly, there will be unpleasant memories about the place. On the second date, you can invite your partner to your favorite restaurant. You will feel comfortable in the usual atmosphere, and the meeting will be easy and relaxed.

4. Karaoke

You don't have to sing, and you don't have to compete with each other. Karaoke is a good theme for a conversation and a way to find out each other's musical preferences.

5. Picnic

An idea similar to the first one on the list. Get ready for the picnic: buy different Goodies, your favorite drinks, prepare the snacks at home. Go to the Park or the autumn forest. Such a date will give you pleasant emotions and show you how much you two are comfortable spending time alone.

6. Dinner at home

An option for those who like to spend their evenings at home. If you are already ready to invite a friend home, you can cook dinner together and watch a romantic movie.

7. Festival street food

Our favorite option. You can try different cuisines, treat each other, find out the taste preferences of the chosen one. At such festivals, it is usually fun, lots of people, songs and dances. You won't get bored in time.

8. Exhibition or the museum

If you two are interested in museums or exhibitions, it's a good idea to meet. Just choose a topic that interests you both: art, history, and science. Wander around the Museum, discuss paintings or installations. After such an interesting date, the partner will definitely want to continue acquaintance ;)
